## Problem

I need to turn on USB debugging.

## Solution

Tested on MI CC 9:

- Settings > My device > All specs > Keep tapping MIUI version til you see "No need, you are already a developer."
- Back to Settings > Additional Settings > Developer options
- Enjoy your time!
